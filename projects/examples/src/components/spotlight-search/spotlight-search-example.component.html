Press Command+f on MacOS / Ctrl+f on PC or click here to open the Spotlight Search.

There are two sections. One does the search asynchronously while the other synchronously.

Use the keyboard arrow keys to move through the search result. You can do this even while the results are still loading.

Press 'Enter' to invoke the selected action. You can also use the mouse to click on an item
